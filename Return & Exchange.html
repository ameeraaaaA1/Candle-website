RETURN POLICY
AIA Candles' online store purchases are categorically non-refundable.
EXCHANGE POLICY

Please be aware that exchanges based on a change of heart are not permitted. Only the conditions listed below apply for exchange requests, which require AIA Candles' approval:

1. Items having manufacturing flaws

2. Exchange requests are only valid for 15 days following the delivery date.

3. Goods are in their original box with all of the tags still attached.
EXCHANGE PROCESS

Customers must submit a form using the Contact Form in order to submit an exchange request. They must include their purchase number, the reason for any returns, and a statement that they are aware of any return delivery fees. To exchange your purchases, kindly refrain from bringing them to AIA candles speciality stores.

After examining the returned goods for manufacturing flaws, AIA Candles will proceed to repay the appropriate items within 14 working days after receiving the returned item/package or inquiry. For direct exchanges, AIA Candles will pay the return shipping costs or make arrangements for a pick-up service. Any returns that don't adhere to the aforementioned requirements may be rejected at AIA Candles' discretion. Items lost or damaged in transit on the way back to us are not the responsibility of AIA Candles.

Email: aiacandles@gmail.com for returns and exchanges.
