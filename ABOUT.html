ABOUT US

 

AiA Candles is a Malaysian-owned artisanal, handmade fragrance business. Our 100% premium soy candles are hand-poured in small batches in Kuantan, Pahang workshop since 2022.

We believe in the art of excellence, and each handpicked aroma is particularly developed for those who value the handcrafted. Our handcrafted soy candles are built around product development.

AiA Candles values painstaking craftsmanship, high-quality materials, and environmentally friendly practises. Our items are gender-neutral and minimalistically designed.

We take pride in providing a bespoke variety of scents that have been carefully selected and tested to have the best formulation of premium essential oils and natural extracts manufactured.

Our eco-friendly and economical candles, made with the highest quality ingredients and packed in visually pleasing packaging, reflect our appreciation for simplicity, design, and function.

We believe that our soy candles will complement your home décor, add warmth to any environment, and brighten your day.

Why soy candles?

  • Less Toxic- Soy wax are less toxic as compared to paraffin candles and are less likely to trigger allergies. Besides that, due to the fact that it has a lower burning point, it is less likely to create an after effect of soot in your candle (the black stuff you see in your regular mass produced candles).

  • Longer Burning- It also has a lower melting point that enables it to burn slower and cleaner than paraffin based candles.

  • Renewable & Biodegradable- Unlike paraffin which comes from petroleum, soy is a renewable plant-derived product.

  • Low Heat – Soy produces less heat than paraffin and keeps your house cooler.
