ORDERING

1) How do I track my order?

Under "My Account," you can monitor the status of your orders. As soon as your order has left the warehouse, an email confirmation will be provided to you as well.

2) Does AIA candles provide Cash On Delivery (COD) service?

Currently, Cash On Delivery (COD) is not an option.

3) How do I cancel order?

Please get in touch with us right away at aiacandles@gmail.com if you need to cancel your order for any reason. Within 5 working days, we will process your order cancellation. To cancel your order, don't go to any AIA Candles retailers.You can mention your order number to us, and we'll try our best to comply with your request.

We are unable to alter or cancel an order after it has begun the shipment or delivery process to you. Any order that seems suspicious or fraudulent may be cancelled at AIA ccandles' discretion. All fraudulent transactions will be reported to the relevant linked authorities or department that will be in responsibility of conducting further inquiries.

4) How can i use my offline membership points?

There isn't yet a membership system available online. You can use your membership points at the AIA Candles location that is closest to you.
